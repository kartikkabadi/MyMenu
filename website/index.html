Does it change my built-in display?

No. MyMonitor only presents external displays for brightness control.

Why is the app not notarized?

This release is made for a free Apple Developer account, so it is ad hoc signed and not notarized. The buyer installer removes the quarantine flag; macOS privacy access remains your choice.
